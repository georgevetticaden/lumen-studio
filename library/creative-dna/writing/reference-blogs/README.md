# Reference Blogs

Place your published blog posts here as voice calibration exemplars.

Lumen's blog-writing skill reads these files to match your writing voice, tone, and structure patterns. The more examples you provide, the more accurately Lumen can reproduce your style.

**Supported formats:** `.md`, `.pdf`, `.txt`

**Recommended:** 5-10 of your best published posts that represent the voice you want Lumen to use.
