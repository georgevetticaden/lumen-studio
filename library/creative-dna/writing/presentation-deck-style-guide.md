## How to Build This Guide

1. **Review your last 3-5 presentations** — what patterns repeat?
2. **Identify your narrative arc** — how do you typically structure the story?
3. **Document your slide conventions** — what makes your slides look like *yours*?
4. **Capture pacing patterns** — how long do you spend per section type?
5. **Test and iterate** — have Lumen build an outline, compare to your real work
