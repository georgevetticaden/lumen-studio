## How to Build This Guide

1. **Gather 5-10 of your best published posts** — save them in `reference-blogs/`
2. **Read them back-to-back** — look for patterns in voice, structure, and pacing
3. **Extract the rules** — fill in each section above with specific patterns and examples
4. **Test it** — have Lumen write a draft using this guide, then compare to your real voice
5. **Iterate** — refine the guide based on where the output diverges from your style
