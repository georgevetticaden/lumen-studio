Life is Beautiful

Our guide through the Beatitudes

The Story

A Jewish Italian father, Guido, is sent to a concentration camp with his young son. To protect his child's innocence, he transforms their nightmare into an elaborate game — embodying sacrificial love to the very end.

Guido is a modern Christ-figure — showing us what the Beatitudes look like when lived in practice, even in humanity's darkest hour.

As we explore each Beatitude, we'll see how Guido — and ultimately Christ himself — embodies these paradoxical paths to joy...
